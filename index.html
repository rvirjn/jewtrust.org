Who We Are

In rural India students are unable to pursue their education and drop their educational aspirations before acquiring the skills & knowledge to build carriers. This is the terrible setback for our country at large. Bank loans for education are also not easily available to all students in need. Often, they lose out good opportunities before they get a loan and if they get a loan, the financial burden puts them under constant stress while studying. The responsibility to better this situation does not lie with the government or banks alone. It is our collective responsibility to help the future of our country and we need to support the students to ensure that they receive the education that they deserve.
